What We Do

School Tours

In an effort to ensure breakthrough, we offer health education, guidance and counselling to children in Schools with the help of well wishers and volunteers. We provide shoes, clothes, sanitary towels and overall health commodities to the least fortunate.

Poverty

We visit children in the Orphanages, elderly homes, prisons, hospitals and people living with disabilities, with an effort to bring hope. Through donations and members contributions, the money raised is used to buy basic needs like food and clothing.

Livelihoods

Through donations and savings, we hope families will be financially stable and able to support and strengthen their communities.

Rescue Shelters

We provide spaces where the least fortunate can have food, shelter and clothing with a hope of building long lasting rescue shelters.

Child Sponsporship

"For only Ksh.50 and Ksh.100 a day you bring a lasting change in a child's life by providing food and access to education programs for a child."
